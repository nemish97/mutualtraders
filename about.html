Mutual Traders is a company which was established in the year 1942 by late Mr. Motiram Arora. Now the company is being run by his son Mr. Manoj Arora and his grandson Mr. Anchit Arora that is the 3rd generation of the company. Mutual Traders is a company founded on three pillars: Quality, Commitment and Reliability . It also mirrors diligence, dedication and 50+ years of herbal market experience of its founder. Our company has been into imports and exports since a very long time. Our customers / client list include people who have been trading with us for more than 50 years. Ever since The company is established it has been trading into agro commodities specializing in herbal raw materials used in ayurvedic, homeopathy, allopathic, and unani system of Medicines, also in perfumery, paint, essence, and various allied industries. We also deals in various kind of gums, food herbs, and spices. Our motto is to grow mutually.
